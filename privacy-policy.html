Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Scanova Lite ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Scanova Lite does not collect, store, or transmit your scanned documents to our servers. All document scanning and PDF generation occurs locally on your device.

2. Camera Permission

The app requires access to your device camera solely for scanning documents. Camera data is processed locally and is not transmitted externally.

3. Advertising

The free version of Scanova Lite uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Please refer to Google Privacy Policy.

4. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed via RevenueCat and the respective app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not store your payment details.

5. Data Storage

Generated PDF files are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your files.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases

7. Children's Privacy

Scanova Lite is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
